Pig Dice Rules

This is a simple dice game. You win by being the first player to score 100 or more points

The rules of the game are:

  • When the player rolls a 2 - 6, the dice score is added to their turn total and they can continue to play.
  • When a player rolls a 1, their turn score reverts to 0 and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • When the player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their overall score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • The player who first scores 100 or more points wins the game.

Congratulations, you win!
